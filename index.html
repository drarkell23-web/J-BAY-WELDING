Structural Insights

The Marine Environment (C5-M)

J-Bay sits in a C5-M corrosive zone. Standard mild steel will fail within 12-24 months without high-specification protection. We utilize Hot-Dip Galvanizing (ISO 1461) and Duplex Stainless Steel for critical load-bearing structures.

Educational Tip: Always verify the micron thickness of your galvanizing. For coastal work, anything less than 85 microns is a liability.
