Before you Order

Don't cheap out on paint

If it's going outside in J-Bay, standard primer won't last a year. Ask for galvanizing or a proper marine-grade coat.

Measurements

Measure twice, cut once. If you're giving us the specs, double-check your inner vs outer dimensions.

Stainless Grades

304 is for indoors. 316 is for the beach. If we’re making something for your boat or sea-front house, go with 316.
